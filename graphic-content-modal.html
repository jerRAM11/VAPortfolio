Project Overview

This project demonstrates my ability to create simple yet effective graphic designs for social media posts, promotional materials, and digital announcements. The work focuses on organizing visual elements to deliver clear, professional, and engaging content.

Operational Problem

Businesses often need visually organized promotional graphics for job hiring announcements and product promotions. Without clear design and layout, information can appear cluttered, making it difficult for audiences to quickly understand the message.

Solution
  • Designed hiring announcement graphics for recruitment posts
  • Created promotional product advertisements
  • Organized text and visual elements for clear communication
  • Edited and resized images for social media platforms
  • Maintained consistent color themes and typography
Process Implementation
  • Prepared content layout before designing graphics
  • Used design tools to arrange visual elements
  • Adjusted image sizes and formatting for platform requirements
  • Ensured content readability and visual balance
Results & Impact
  • Produced visually organized social media graphics
  • Improved clarity of promotional and recruitment messages
  • Delivered ready-to-use digital materials for posting
  • Maintained consistent and professional visual presentation
